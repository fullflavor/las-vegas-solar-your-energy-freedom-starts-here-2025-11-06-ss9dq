Solar panels generate electricity even on cloudy days, though at reduced efficiency compared to sunny conditions. During nighttime, your system draws electricity from the grid as normal. If you want energy independence 24/7, we offer battery storage solutions that store excess daytime energy for nighttime use. Additionally, Las Vegas receives an average of 310 sunny days per year, making it ideal for solar production. Our systems are designed to maximize performance throughout all seasons and weather conditions.

Las Vegas solar potential: 5.5+ peak sun hours daily average

We understand that financing is crucial to making solar accessible. We offer multiple options to fit your needs: Solar Loans (own the system, get tax credits), Solar Leases (low upfront costs, predictable payments), Power Purchase Agreements (pay only for energy produced), Cash Purchases (maximize savings), and specialty programs for military members and seniors. Many of our customers qualify for zero-down financing options. We also help you take advantage of the federal 30% Investment Tax Credit and Nevada state incentives to maximize your financial benefits.

Federal tax credit: 30% of installation costs | Multiple financing options available

Solar systems require minimal maintenance, which is one of their greatest advantages. Most systems need only occasional cleaning (2-4 times per year) to remove dust and debris, especially in the desert environment. We recommend professional cleaning annually to optimize performance. Modern inverters are solid-state with no moving parts, and panels have no mechanical components to wear out. Our monitoring system alerts us to any issues immediately, and we provide proactive maintenance checks. Most customers enjoy decades of trouble-free operation with minimal intervention. We include maintenance support in our service packages.

Maintenance: Minimal | Professional cleaning: 1-2 times annually recommended
